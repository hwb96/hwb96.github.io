I develop LLM agent/workflow systems and retrieval pipelines for clinical-domain applications. Recent work includes stateful graph orchestration (routing, loops, retries, state), tool use with strict structured I/O contracts, hybrid retrieval + reranking, citation grounding, and evaluation harnesses for regression and risk control. I have also worked on large-model training and compliance filing. See [CV](/cv/) and [Blog](/blog/) for details.

我目前在杭州一家临床医学相关的 CRO 公司做算法研发，方向是大模型智能体/工作流与检索系统。近期工作包括有状态图编排（路由、循环、重试、状态管理）、工具调用的结构化协议与校验、混合检索与重排、引用对齐与可追溯，以及评测集与自动回归体系建设，用工程化的方式控制高风险错误。我也参与过基座模型训练与合规备案。更多信息见 [CV](/cv/) 与 [Blog](/blog/)。
